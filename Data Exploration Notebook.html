Loading data and simple statistics¶

In this dataset we have the column "Churn" is the target class, which take value "Yes" or "No". "Yes" mean the customer is going to churn next month, "No" mean he/she stay.

The dataset also has an ID column named "customerID" and one encoded categorical column "SeniorCitizen" in which 1 stand for Yes, and 0 stand for No.

A simple information about the dataset is given as follow:

Summary:¶

  • The customers who churn in dataset tend to have very low tenure, medium -> high MonthlyCharges and very low TotalCharges.

  • The Churn group is dense when the non-churn group is very spread out on numerical variables.

  • TotalCharges variables should be scaled before using. MonthlyCharges variables should be converted into 3 bins: Low Charges (<40), Medium Charges (40-60), High Charges (>60). Tenure is an ordinal variables and shouble be converted to 4 bins: New Customer (<12), Bronze Customer (12-24), Gold Customer (24-60), Platium Customer (>60)

  • Customer with the month-to-month contract has higher probability to churn than other type of contract.

  • Customer who received no Tech support has higher probability to churn than customer has tech support.

  • Customer who pay by electronic check has higher probability to churn than customer has tech support.
